Remote Area Medical at Cornell (RAM Cornell)

Bringing free quality dental and medical services to underserved communities.

About Us

RAM Cornell is the official Cornell University chapter of Remote Area Medical, a national nonprofit providing free pop-up clinics across the U.S.

Our Mission

We aim to increase access to health services, especially dental care, for low-income communities by coordinating student volunteers to assist at RAM clinics and organizing public health outreach on campus.
